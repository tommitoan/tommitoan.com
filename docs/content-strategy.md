# Content Strategy

## Content principle

The site should feel curated, not exhaustive.

Only show content that supports one of these jobs:

- establish identity
- prove technical depth
- showcase live work
- invite exploration
- route users to the right destination

## Content by area

### Homepage

Must include:

- short personal identity statement
- 3 path choices
- very short explanation of each path
- subtle proof that this is more than a portfolio

Must avoid:

- full resume sections
- long skill grids
- giant wall of cards

### Discover

Should include:

- short narrative about who you are beyond job title
- homelab and k3s story
- self-hosted services overview at a conceptual level
- personal content channels
- selected playgrounds and experiments
- current focus or current builds

Potential content blocks:

- `Homelab at a glance`
- `What I self-host`
- `Things I am exploring`
- `Videos and social content`
- `Playgrounds`

### Products

Should include:

- Bazica
- blog platform when available
- public utilities
- product cards with status labels

Suggested status labels:

- `Live`
- `Preview`
- `Private`
- `Planned`

## Content model recommendation

Use a single structured content source with these groups:

- `siteMeta`
- `gatewayPanels`
- `discoverSections`
- `productCards`
- `channelLinks`
- `publicServiceCards`
- `footerLinks`

## Writing tone

- personal but not diary-like
- technical but not dense
- confident without sounding corporate
- warm and curious
- short, sharp copy on top-level pages

## Reuse guidance

Reuse from `toanngo.cv`:

- strongest project proof
- engineering credibility
- current role and identity lines

Do not duplicate heavily from `toanngo.cv`:

- full experience timeline
- full skills inventory
- full resume details

Instead, link out when visitors want the professional deep dive.

## Priority backlog for content gathering

Phase 1 required:

- final panel names and short descriptions
- short bio for homepage
- Bazica product card
- homelab summary copy
- links to current public channels

Phase 2 desirable:

- screenshots and diagrams
- current self-hosted service list that is safe to share
- launch notes for upcoming blog and tools

Phase 3 later:

- case studies
- writing archive
- changelog or now page
